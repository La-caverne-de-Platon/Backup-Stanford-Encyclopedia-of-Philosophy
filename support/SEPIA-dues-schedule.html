SEPIA Dues Schedule

SEPIA Dues Schedule for U.S. and Canadian Libraries

The following amounts are for a one-time payment of membership dues to join SEPIA. These amounts can be paid over three years.

•Institutions Granting a Ph.D. in Philosophy:$15,750
•Institutions Granting an M.A. in Philosophy:$6,300
•Institutions Granting a B.A. in Philosophy:$3,150
