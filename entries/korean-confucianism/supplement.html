    • Yi Sŭnghun (1756–1801)
    • Chŏng Yagyong (1762–1836) [Tasan/Dasan]
      • Chungyong kangŭibo (中庸講義補) [Supplement to Lectures on the Doctrine of the Mean]
      • Maengja yoŭi (孟子要義) [The Essentials of The Mencius]

      • Kyŏngse yup’yo (經世遺表) [Design for Good Government]

      • Sipkyemyŏng-ga (십계명가) [Hymn in Praise of the Ten Commandments]
    • Chŏng Yakchong (1760–1801)
      • Chugyo yoji (주교요지) [The Essence of The Lord’s Teachings]
